The most directly relevant comps are the Warp Graphics comic lots (#1-20 range) and the Blood of Ten Chiefs paperback anthology lots. For the Warp comic run (#1-20), the clearest comp is $25.53 (21 issues + Summer Special, Apr 2026) and $50.42 (20 issues, Mar 2026), suggesting a working range of roughly $25–$50 for a partial-to-full Warp comic run landed. For the paperback anthology lots (Tor Books, prose anthologies), 2-volume sets land around $19–$33 and 3-4 volume sets around $33–$40. Two outliers should be excluded from the working range: the 43-issue ElfQuest mega-lot ($150.64) and the mint complete ElfQuest series ($316.53) — both are large mixed collections far beyond a Blood of Ten Chiefs lot. The June 2020 single-issue comps are 180+ days stale and serve as floor/reference only. Recommended list price for a standard Blood of Ten Chiefs Warp comic lot (#1-20, VF/NM) is **$35–$45 landed**; for the prose paperback anthology lot (vols 1-4), **$30–$40 landed**. Price at the lower end given the thin directly-relevant comp pool and the broader market softness on 1990s Warp indie titles.
